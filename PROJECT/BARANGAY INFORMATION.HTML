About Barangay Poblacion

Our History

Barangay Poblacion has been the heart of our community for over 50 years. Originally established in 1970, it has grown from a small rural settlement to a thriving urban community with over 5,000 residents.

Our barangay is known for its strong sense of community, active civic participation, and commitment to sustainable development. We pride ourselves on maintaining our cultural heritage while embracing modern innovations.

Under the leadership of our dedicated officials, we have implemented numerous programs to improve the quality of life for all residents, including health services, educational support, and environmental initiatives.
