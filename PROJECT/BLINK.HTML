About BLINK

Founded in 2023, BLINK is on a mission to help people preserve their most precious memories and thoughts for future discovery. Our innovative time-based social platform allows users to create digital time capsules that unlock at predetermined dates.

We believe that the most meaningful moments in life deserve to be preserved and rediscovered at the perfect time. Whether it's a message to your future self, a shared memory with loved ones, or a community milestone, BLINK provides the perfect vessel for time-traveling memories.

Our Mission

To create meaningful connections between past and future selves, fostering reflection, anticipation, and personal growth through time-based digital experiences.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted platform for preserving and sharing time-capsule memories, connecting generations and creating lasting digital legacies.
