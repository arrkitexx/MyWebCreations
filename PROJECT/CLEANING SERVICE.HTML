Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What cleaning products do you use?

We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for your family and pets. All our products are biodegradable and environmentally responsible.

How do I schedule a cleaning service?

You can schedule a cleaning service by calling us, using our online booking form, or through our mobile app. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance.

Are your cleaners insured and background-checked?

Yes, all our cleaners are fully insured and have undergone thorough background checks. Your safety and security are our top priorities.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, please contact us within 24 hours, and we'll re-clean at no additional cost.
