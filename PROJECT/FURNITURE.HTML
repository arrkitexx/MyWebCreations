About ARKITEXX

Founded in the heart of Dumaguete, ARKITEXX is dedicated to preserving traditional Filipino craftsmanship while creating modern, functional furniture and handicrafts. Our artisans combine time-honored techniques with contemporary design to create pieces that tell a story.

Each piece in our collection is handcrafted by skilled local artisans who have been perfecting their craft for generations. We believe in sustainable practices and use locally sourced materials whenever possible.

From bamboo lounges to mango wood dining tables, our furniture is designed to last a lifetime and become a cherished part of your home.
