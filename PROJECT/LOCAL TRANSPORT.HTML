About ARKITEXX

Founded in 2010, ARKITEXX has been providing reliable transportation services across Negros Oriental and Occidental for over a decade. Our commitment to safety, comfort, and punctuality has made us the preferred choice for travelers.

We operate modern, well-maintained vehicles with professional drivers who prioritize your safety and comfort. Our routes cover major destinations including Dumaguete, Bacolod, Cebu, Roxas, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your luggage policy?

Each passenger is allowed one medium-sized suitcase (not exceeding 20kg) and one small hand carry. Additional or oversized luggage may incur extra charges.

Can I bring my pet?

Small pets in carriers are allowed. Please inform us during booking so we can accommodate your pet properly. Service animals are always welcome.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available if requested 24 hours before departure. A 15% cancellation fee applies. No refunds for no-shows or late arrivals.

How do I track my trip status?

You can check trip status updates through our website or by calling our hotline. We'll also send SMS notifications for any delays or changes.
